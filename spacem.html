What is this project?

This is a office space ship, where they do their research and build robots, which are all made up of modular assets, modeled by me! The modular assets are barrels, wires, glowing tubes, chairs and tables, railings, walls of office, walls of alleyway, metal floor where the robot is located, lights. they were all made within Maya.

There are also VFX involved, making dust, sparks and glowing particles, done by me.

Hero Asset

The robot here was made without modular assets, which was modeled in maya and textured in Substance Painter.
