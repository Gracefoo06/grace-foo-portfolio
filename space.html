What is Space Trails?

In this first-person, collecting, adveture game, you are lost on a mysterious planet and your ship is broken. Its up to you to explore this mysterious planet to collect 5 things to fix your ship.

I coded the whole game by myself

modeled the structure of the space ship, the big yellow shrine, and the collectable models ( the crystal, nail, screw, screw driver, door knob ) other models were borrowed from Unity Asset Store or sketchfab.

I did the the fire and poision VFX

I did the UI for the game.

Level Design

Level Abstract

  1. Crashed space ship, no enemy (start). The portals in the ship are also broken.
  2. The player goes out of ship to find things to help fix the ship, like the restaurant for the hammer, and the shop for a screwdriver.
  3. Japanese golden shrine, surrounded by water. When walking up to it, lava suddenly rises.
  4. Go into the shrine where the interior of the shrine is chaotic, reflecting the fox god’s magic and personality.
  5. Inside the shrine, either walk through the fire or jump over lava with floating rocks.
  6. Go through a maze and avoid poison traps.
  7. You finish the fox god’s trial and they give you a crystal to fix your ship. You go behind to find a portal, fix it, and teleport back to your ship.
  8. Teleport to your generator and fix it, then the ship flies off (end).
