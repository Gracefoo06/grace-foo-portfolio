What are these projects?

These are items I've designed for a module called 3D Prototyping, where I had to design, prototype and research into making 3d products, such as practical items, decoration and figures. I've received an award for best performance in this module.

Laser Cut and 3D Printed Decorations for project JourneyX

I desgined decorations inspired by my project JourneyX which I worked with NHB to create. I laser cutted designs of the stops, the mascot standees, and the logo, which were all done in Adobe Illustrator. The rest of the items such as the mascot figures and the light-up runway box are 3d printed. They were designed within Maya. The final design document for this project can be seen here.

3D Light-up Acrlic Box with custom designs

I desgined a box that lights up on your desk that can also help sort all your items on your desk with a filter and non filter lid. In the front you can place your favourite laser cut designs, pictures, your phone and you can even hang keychains using a special panel. I used Adobe Illustrator to design the later cut pieces which can be seen here. The design document for this project can be seen here.

3D Printed Axelotl Figure

I 3d modelled and designed a 3d figure of a mascot of a youtuber named Axel Syrios, where the mascot is named an "Axelotl". It has movable parts such as the arms legs and head. Additionally it has removable parts where you can deocrate the axelotl in different expressions and accessories here.
