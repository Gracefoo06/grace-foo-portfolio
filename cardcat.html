What is Sushi Wars?

I had to make a card and board game, thus I designed a game called Sushi Wars where cats had to bid to get the highest amount of sushi stars, either by buying a lot of cheaper sushi low stared fish or keeping a few high quality high stared fish.

Players would lay their fish cards they owned on the tables infront of the cats, on the game mat. I also designed the back of the game mat

I created different fishes of different quality and the back of the cards
