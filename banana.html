What is Banana Smasher?

Banana smasher is a game where you can log in and smash as many bananas as you can within a minute. Your progress is saved through a database system called Firebase, where you can also see your score on the leaderboard.

I coded the whole game by myself

I modeled the bananas

I did the UI and art for the game.

Firebase/database features the game has

  • Sign up
  • Sign In
  • Forget Password
  • Log Out
  • Update Username
  • Delete Score
  • Update's score when new high score
  • Leaderboard ranked checking data from database
