What is this project?

I created an AR portfolio, using Unity and Vulforia showing off my previous works through AR, where you can move around models to take a better look ( which could leave a secret to be unfolded ) and even get to know me better. I designed the UI and the art in the game and there is even a quiz at the start to make learning about me even more fun.
